Yuanlei Niu is currently undergraduate student of the final year at Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University. She serves as the group leader in Beijing and researcher of Abundy Inc..

Yuanlei Niu received various awards and scholarships in Tsinghua University. She was one of thirty recipients of Qualcomm Scholarship, Huawei Scholarship winner and National Endeavor Scholarship winner. She was also awarded for Academic Excellence in 2015 and 2016. She took part in many science competitons and won excellent prizes. She won the first prize in CUPT and Honorable Mention in MCM/ICM.

Yuanlei Niu graduated from Zhengzhou No.1 Senior High School. She had won many national awards in math Olympics before entering Tsinghua University. She was a member of Tsinghua Leader Plan and became an A-class member for less than 30 students nationwide.

Yuanlei Niu is expert at swimming, basketball, ping-pang, badminton and tennis. Previously, in the most famous competitions in Tsinghua, John Ma Cup, she represented Department of Electronic Engineering to take part in swimming competitions and won silver medal in Women’s 200m Breaststroke and bronze medal in Women’s 100m Breasrstroke. She also serves as the first enter in Basketball Team of the Department and won bronze medal in John Ma Cup.
