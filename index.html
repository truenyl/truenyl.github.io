Welcome to my personal page! I currently serve as the Head of AI at Century Games, where I am exploring the vast potential of AI in gaming, a field I believe is key to the future. Prior to joining Century Games, I was an Investment Associate at GSR Ventures, focusing on investments in AI-native SaaS and applications.

I earned my Master's degree in Software Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University in 2019, and a Bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering (with honor) and Economics from Tsinghua University in 2018.

I have a profound passion for mathematics, which led me to participate in and win a gold medal in the Math Olympiad during my high school years.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I am an avid sports enthusiast. I excel in swimming, table tennis, badminton, and tennis. I have represented the Electronic Engineering Department's swimming team at Tsinghua University in the "John Mo Cup," securing silver in the 200m breaststroke and bronze in the 100m breaststroke.
