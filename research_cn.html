研究经历

  • Group Leader and Researcher, Abundy Inc., Beijing Research Group, 2017.9-Present
    Advisor: Professor Hao Zou & Professor Min Wang
    Project : User Action Prediction on Alipay
    Applied LSTM models on financial data to alleviate the burden of feature engineering step.
    Implemented the simulation environment for Generative Adversarial Imitation Learning.
    AppliedGenerativeAdversarialImitationLearningalgorithmtopredictusers’purchasesandredemption.
  • Research Assistant, Department of Electrical Engineering, Stanford University, 2017.6-2017.8
    Advisor: Professor (visiting) Hao Zou & Professor John M. Cioffi
    Project : Credit Card Fraud Detection Based on Artificial Intelligence
    Applied SVMs, gradient boosted trees, logistic regression, random forests and neural networks models in credit card fraud detection to avoid time-consuming manual work.
    Deployed proposed models on Spark to deal with big data problems.
    Enhanced the detection precision from 6% to 10% on real-time transactions from one of the biggest joint-stock banks in China.
  • Research Assistant, Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, 2017.5
    Advisor: Associate Professor Yong Li
    Course Project: Message Broadcasting on the Social Network
    Crawled data from weibo, Chinese biggest social network.
    Analyzed the message broadcasting pattern of false information from million times of forwarding with modified Louvain algorithm.
  • Research Assistant, Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, 2017.3-2017.5
    Advisor: Associate Professor Yu Wang
    Project: Research on Community Detection Algorithm
    Modified existing Louvain algorithm and decreased the complexity to nearly-linear complexity on large sparse graphs.
    Implemented the algorithm on distributed computing system with graph partitioning methods in Scala.
  • Research Assistant, Center for FinTech, Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University, 2016.9-2017.1
    Advisor: Chair Professor Hao Zou
    Project : Lung Nodule Detection and Classification
    Utilized transfer learning with a 50-layer residual network for nodule classification.
    Proposed data augmentation methods to increase the number of training images based on speciality in size and location of nodules due to the inadequacy the original dataset.
    Enhanced nodule detection sensitivity from 80% (existing Computer Aided Detection systems) to 92%.
  • Visiting Student Researcher, Storage System Research Center, University of California, Santa Cruz, 2016.6-2016.8
    Advisor: Assistant Professor Jishen Zhao
    Project : Eﬃcient Caching Policy Design for NVRAM-Based Storage Systems
    Programmed a benchmark for persistent memory and non-persistent memory.
    Tested the property of different memories given different test sets and analyzed the reason of the performance.
    Assisted in designing a new storage system based on high-performance computing and energy efficiency.
